Our Mission

Bringing Transparency to the Medical School Journey

We believe that every aspiring physician deserves access to authentic insights, real experiences, and a supportive community during the high-stakes application and interview process.

Our Story

menu_book

The Problem with the Process

For decades, the medical school application process has been shrouded in mystery. Students relied on rumors, fragmented forums, and expensive consultants. We saw a system that favored connections over raw talent and decided it was time to level the playing field.

10k+ Interview Reports

Empowering the next generation of physicians through shared knowledge.

lightbulb

The Turning Point

DocStory was born from a simple idea: what if every applicant could read a detailed, honest review of the interview day before they stepped foot on campus? We started by collecting stories from our peers, and the response was overwhelming.

diversity_3

Building the Community

Today, we are a thriving community of thousands of pre-meds and current medical students. We don't just share interview questions; we share the vibe, the culture, and the reality of medical education.
